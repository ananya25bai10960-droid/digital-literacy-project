Part B. PREVENTION CHECKLIST

*🛡️ Stay Safe Online – Prevention Tips*

1. Never share OTPs, PINs, passwords with others  
2. Always use strong and separate passwords for all accounts  
3. Always use two-factor authentication when available  
4. Never click on suspicious links and pop-up messages  
5. Never share your personal information on unverified websites  
6. Always check bank statements and transaction alerts  
7. Never use public Wi-Fi for UPI transactions and other financial activities  
8. Always log out after using public devices

*💰 UPI / Financial Safety Tips*

* Never approve any UPI collect requests from unknown people  
* Always verify the name of the receiver before making any transaction
