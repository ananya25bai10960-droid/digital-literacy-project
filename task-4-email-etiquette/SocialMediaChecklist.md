# Part B. Social Media Checklist

✅ DO’s

* *Set high privacy settings for all social media accounts*  
* *Share respectful and appropriate content*  
* *Be thoughtful before posting or commenting on social media*  
* *Use professional language for academic and professional platforms*  
* *Report fake accounts and abusive activities*





  
**❌ DON’Ts**

* *Avoid sharing personal information like address, OTPs, and bank details*  
* *Avoid online harassment and hate speech*  
* *Avoid clicking on suspicious links and messages*  
* *Avoid oversharing personal life on social media*  
* *Avoid posting content that can negatively impact your future career*
